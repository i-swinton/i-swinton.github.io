Headcannon

A multiplayer game where players battle against one another to claim the high score.

Headcannon is a party multiplayer game where players compete against one another by firing at each other to rack up points, with the help of some wacky power ups.

Develpment

This game was created by a small team of high school developers during a high school game jam.
